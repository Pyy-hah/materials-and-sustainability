Comprehensive Life Cycle Analysis of Gym Vests

Background

With the increasing awareness of sustainable development and environmental protection, the textile industry is also working to reduce its environmental impact. Some textile companies have begun to adopt more environmentally friendly production methods, such as using renewable energy, reducing the use of chemicals, and promoting circular economy. At the same time, some manufacturers are also promoting the use of organic cotton, recycled fibers and other environmentally friendly materials. The entire industry chain is working to reduce carbon emissions, reduce wastewater emissions, and improve resource efficiency to achieve sustainable development. The gym vests in this survey are usually made of high-stretch polyester fabrics, which have good elasticity and durability, and are easy to dye, so they are favored by consumers and producers.
